Security & Privacy

This page outlines the core security and privacy principles of the BBS platform.

Prototype notice: This content is conceptual and provided for transparency. It does not represent an operational or certified security system.

Principles

  • Data minimization and clear processing purposes.
  • Human rights and dignity by design.
  • Access control based on necessity.

Practices (Conceptual)

  • Encryption in transit and at rest.
  • Separation of duties and auditability.
  • Regular review of configurations.

Processes

  • Incident awareness and transparent communication.
  • Risk assessment and continuous improvement.
  • Clear documentation and accountability.
