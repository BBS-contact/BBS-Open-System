Authenticity & Human Dignity

A foundational value of the BBS platform.

Authenticity means that every person interacting with the BBS platform is recognized as a real human being — not as a data object, profile, or resource.

BBS is designed to protect human dignity, freedom of thought, and the right to personal identity, without manipulation, coercion, or hidden influence.

Systems built within BBS must always prioritize truth, responsibility, and respect over efficiency, profit, or control.

What Authenticity Means in Practice

  • No hidden behavioral manipulation
  • No artificial pressure or psychological exploitation
  • No reduction of people to scores or abstract labels
  • Clear distinction between human decisions and automated processes

Human Dignity as a Non-Negotiable Principle

Any system that compromises human dignity, even in the name of optimization or security, contradicts the core values of BBS.

Technology must remain a tool that serves people — never a force that replaces or dominates them.

This page expresses the core values of the BBS platform. It does not constitute a legal, contractual, or policy document.
