# Strategic Alignment Brief  
Fundacja BBS — Better Balance System  
2026

## 1. Purpose

This document outlines how the research activities of Fundacja BBS — Better Balance System align with selected European Union priorities in digital governance, AI regulation, and transparency-oriented digital transformation.

---

## 2. Alignment with EU Digital Strategy

The Foundation’s research supports objectives consistent with:

- Digital Europe Programme priorities,
- European strategy for data,
- Trustworthy AI development principles,
- Public-sector digital transformation initiatives.

The project contributes to structured experimentation in governance-support architectures rather than proposing institutional replacement models.

---

## 3. Alignment with the EU Artificial Intelligence Act

LEO development incorporates:

- risk-based AI classification awareness,
- human oversight integration,
- documentation traceability,
- structured logging mechanisms,
- compliance-conscious design.

The system is conceived as an advisory framework and does not operate as a fully autonomous decision-making system.

---

## 4. GDPR and Data Governance Considerations

Development activities consider:

- proportional data processing,
- minimization principles,
- structured audit-readiness,
- traceability of decision-support pathways.

Pilot frameworks are designed to include prior data protection review and compliance assessment.

---

## 5. Public-Sector Relevance

The research initiative may support:

- documentation harmonization efforts,
- structured workflow modeling,
- audit-support experimentation,
- AI governance sandbox-style pilots.

Implementation remains conditional upon regulatory feasibility and institutional partnership agreements.

---

## 6. Research Positioning

The Foundation positions its work as:

- laboratory-based governance research,
- compliance-aware digital architecture modeling,
- modular experimentation under controlled pilot conditions.

The initiative does not claim systemic reform mandates and remains within research and advisory boundaries.
