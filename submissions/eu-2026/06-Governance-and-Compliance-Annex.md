# Governance and Compliance Annex  
Fundacja BBS — Better Balance System  
2026

## 1. Legal Status

Fundacja BBS — Better Balance System is a registered Polish non-profit foundation operating under Polish foundation law and applicable European Union regulations.

The Foundation maintains:

- Statutory governance structure  
- Accounting procedures in accordance with Polish law  
- Defined Management Board responsibilities  
- Compliance-aware operational documentation  

---

## 2. Governance Structure

The Foundation operates under:

- A formal statute  
- Defined Management Board authority  
- Documented decision-making procedures  
- Accountability for financial and operational actions  

All activities are executed within statutory boundaries.

---

## 3. Operational Responsibility

The Foundation maintains:

- Clear designation of responsible persons  
- Documented internal procedures  
- Structured record-keeping  
- Version-controlled documentation practices  

No automated system replaces institutional accountability.

Human authority remains primary.

---

## 4. Regulatory Compliance

Development and pilot research activities consider alignment with:

- Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR)  
- EU Artificial Intelligence Act  
- EU data governance principles  
- Polish accounting and reporting requirements  

Data protection and regulatory feasibility are reviewed prior to pilot implementation.

---

## 5. Risk Management

The Foundation acknowledges:

- Regulatory evolution  
- Institutional variability  
- Technical limitations  
- Context-dependent deployment constraints  

Risk mitigation includes:

- Incremental pilot scope  
- Compliance checkpoints  
- Documentation traceability  
- Human oversight integration

## 6. Ethical Positioning

The initiative is structured as:

- Research-oriented  
- Advisory in function  
- Compliance-aware  
- Proportionate in scope  

The Foundation does not claim institutional replacement mandates or systemic transformation authority.

---

## 7. Transparency and Reporting

The Foundation commits to:

- Annual reporting as required by law  
- Financial transparency within statutory obligations  
- Clear separation of research and operational activities  
- Documented governance oversight
