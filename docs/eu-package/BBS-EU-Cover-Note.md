### Subject

---

### Purpose of This Submission  
The enclosed documents present BBS (Big Begins Small), an ethical, transparent, tamper-proof digital ecosystem designed to strengthen:

- public trust in institutions  
- transparency and accountability  
- responsible AI use  
- cross-border digital cooperation  
- integrity of decision-making processes  

The goal of this submission is **not financial support**, but:

1. institutional dialogue;  
2. scientific evaluation by STOA and JRC;  
3. exploration of a controlled pilot within an EU institution.

---

### Summary of Request  
We respectfully request that the European Commission:

1. acknowledges receipt of this package;  
2. initiates a formal evaluation process;  
3. facilitates scientific review by the relevant EU bodies;
