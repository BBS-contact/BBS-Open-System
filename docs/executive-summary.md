# Executive Summary  
Fundacja BBS — Better Balance System

## 1. Institutional Overview

Fundacja BBS — Better Balance System is a Polish non-profit foundation registered in the National Court Register (KRS 0001220296).

The Foundation operates as a research-oriented and governance-focused institution dedicated to developing and evaluating digital systems that enhance transparency, accountability, and ethical AI integration within institutional environments.

Its activities are aligned with European regulatory frameworks and conducted under Polish law.

---

## 2. Context

Public institutions across Europe face increasing complexity in:

- digital transformation,
- interoperability of data systems,
- AI integration in administrative processes,
- transparency and documentation standards,
- safeguarding fundamental rights in digital environments.

There is growing demand for structured, compliance-oriented digital frameworks capable of supporting responsible innovation.

---

## 3. Mission

The Foundation’s mission is to:

- conduct research in governance-support technologies,
- develop ethical AI-based analytical tools,
- explore transparency-enhancing digital architectures,
- promote structured documentation and auditability,
- support public-interest digital innovation.

The Foundation does not exercise public authority and does not replace governmental institutions. Its systems function in advisory and research capacities.

---

## 4. Core Research Areas

The Foundation’s work includes:

- AI governance and risk-based analytical systems (LEO),
- documentation and traceability models,
- modular digital infrastructure design,
- institutional transparency frameworks,
- compliance-oriented system architecture,
- interdisciplinary collaboration with academic and professional partners.

---

## 5. Distinguishing Features

Fundacja BBS — Better Balance System emphasizes:

- regulatory alignment (GDPR, EU AI Act),
- human oversight in AI-supported processes,
- proportionate transparency mechanisms,
- structured audit and documentation workflows,
- modular and research-scalable architecture.

The Foundation prioritizes institutional integrity and procedural clarity rather than political or commercial objectives.

---

## 6. Strategic Outlook

The Foundation seeks to contribute to European discussions on:

- trustworthy AI,
- digital governance,
- institutional resilience,
- public-sector innovation,
- ethical digital transformation.

Future activities may include pilot research collaborations, academic partnerships, and structured innovation projects within lawful and regulatory frameworks.

---

## 7. Conclusion

Fundacja BBS — Better Balance System operates as a research-driven, compliance-aware institution exploring responsible digital infrastructure models.

Its objective is not to replace institutions, but to support evidence-based, transparent, and ethically grounded digital transformation processes.
