Transparency Statement

BBS is fully open, accountable, and verifiable. We publish integrity proofs for everyone to check.

1. Daily Integrity Proofs

Every day, BBS publishes a Merkle root of verified files and actions. Anyone can independently confirm the integrity of the system.

2. Change Log

All legal, technical, and governance changes are recorded in the Change Log, which is publicly available to all.

3. Independent Oversight

BBS encourages external review and welcomes independent auditors to verify its operations and fairness.

4. Public Accountability

No hidden decisions, no secret deals, no corruption. BBS operates on truth, transparency, and fairness as non-negotiable principles.

5. Updates

This statement may evolve with BBS. All changes are published transparently in the Change Log.
