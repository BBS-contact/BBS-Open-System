# Funding and Collaboration Framework  
Fundacja BBS — Better Balance System

## 1. Purpose

This document defines the funding principles, partnership structure, and collaboration model of Fundacja BBS — Better Balance System.

The Foundation operates as a Polish non-profit research entity. All funding and cooperation activities are conducted in accordance with its statutory objectives and applicable law.

---

## 2. Funding Principles

The Foundation follows these principles:

- Legal compliance under Polish and EU law
- Transparency of financial reporting
- Institutional independence
- Public-interest orientation
- Documented allocation of funds

Financial support does not grant governance control unless explicitly defined by formal agreement.

---

## 3. Areas Supported by Funding

Funding may support:

- Research and development of governance-support technologies
- AI risk and compliance research
- Documentation and transparency tools
- Pilot research projects
- Infrastructure and operational costs
- External audits and expert reviews
- Academic and interdisciplinary collaboration

All allocations are determined by the Management Board under statutory rules.

---

## 4. Institutional Collaboration

The Foundation may cooperate with:

- Academic institutions
- Public-sector research bodies
- Non-profit organizations
- Technical and compliance experts
- EU and international grant programs

Cooperation is governed by written agreements and subject to compliance review.

---

## 5. Pilot and Research Partnerships

Collaboration may include:

- Limited-scope pilot research
- Methodological evaluation
- Structured co-development of research prototypes
- Documentation and compliance modeling initiatives

All pilot cooperation follows the Pilot Research Framework.

---

## 6. Contribution and Participation

The Foundation welcomes structured contributions from:

- Researchers
- Technical experts
- Legal and compliance professionals
- Documentation specialists

All contributions undergo internal review and must align with:

- Applicable law
- GDPR requirements
- Ethical governance standards
- Institutional risk assessment

Sensitive system components remain protected intellectual property.

---

## 7. Intellectual Property

Unless otherwise agreed:

- Contributors retain ownership of their original work.
- The Foundation receives necessary usage rights for integration.
- Licensing terms are defined in formal agreements.

---

## 8. Governance Boundaries

Funding or cooperation does not automatically grant:

- Voting rights
- Governance control
- System-level authority
- Administrative override capabilities

All authority structures are defined by statute and formal agreements.

---

## 9. Financial and Reporting Governance

The Foundation:

- Maintains accounting in accordance with Polish law
- Prepares annual financial statements
- Complies with grant-specific reporting requirements
- Separates statutory and economic activity where applicable
