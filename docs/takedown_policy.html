Takedown Policy

Last updated: October 2025

This Takedown Policy applies to the BBS (Big Begins Small) platform as a conceptual and technical initiative. It does not imply the existence of a registered legal entity and is intended to ensure transparency, fairness, and compliance with applicable law.

1. Purpose

This policy defines how BBS handles requests for the removal (“takedown”) of content or data, balancing freedom of expression with the protection of rights, privacy, and legal obligations.

2. Scope

  • Content published on BBS-managed modules and services.
  • User-generated or third-party materials hosted by BBS.
  • Repositories, libraries, and public records operated under BBS.

3. Grounds for Takedown

  • Copyright infringement or unlawful use.
  • Privacy or data protection violations (GDPR).
  • Defamation or demonstrably harmful content.
  • Security risks (malware, exploits, credential leaks).
  • Legal obligations or valid court orders.
  • Violations of core BBS principles and policies.

4. Request Procedure

A takedown request should include:

  1. Identification of the material (URL or reference).
  2. The legal or policy grounds for removal.
  3. Supporting evidence or explanation.
  4. Contact details of the requester.

Requests should be sent to: bbs.contactproton.me@proton.me with the subject line: “Takedown Request — [Reason]”.

5. Processing of Requests

  • Acknowledgment: Requests are acknowledged within a reasonable time.
  • Review: Requests are evaluated by designated reviewers.
  • Decision: Content may be removed, restricted, or retained with justification.
  • Notification: Relevant parties are informed of the outcome.

6. Exceptions

  • Content required to remain available under applicable law.
  • Content forming part of anonymized transparency or audit archives.
  • Manifestly unfounded, abusive, or fraudulent requests.

7. Appeals

Decisions may be appealed within a reasonable appeal period by providing additional evidence or arguments. Appeals are reviewed independently to ensure fairness and proportionality.

8. Transparency

BBS publishes anonymized statistics on takedown requests and outcomes through its Transparency Portal or Change Log.

9. Contact

For all takedown-related inquiries, contact: bbs.contactproton.me@proton.me
