# Legal Office (Prototype)

**Status:** Demo / Prototype  
**Purpose:** UI showcase only  
**Disclaimer:** Not legal advice

---

## Overview

The **Legal Office (Prototype)** is a demonstration module within the BBS platform.
It showcases how laws and regulations could be structured, browsed, and compared
in a transparent, user-friendly interface.

This module is presented as a **prototype only**.  
All data, analyses, and texts are **example-based** and **non-authoritative**.

---

## What This Prototype Demonstrates

- A **Laws Registry** with search and filtering
- A **Law Detail** page with:
  - overview
  - example text excerpts
  - demo analytical notes
  - version history (illustrative)
- Clear separation between **UI concepts** and **core logic**
- Emphasis on transparency, clarity, and accessibility

---

## What This Prototype Does NOT Include

- No real legal databases
- No automated legal analysis or decision-making
- No legal services or legal advice
- No jurisdiction-specific guarantees
- No backend, APIs, or production data
- No representation of a registered legal entity
