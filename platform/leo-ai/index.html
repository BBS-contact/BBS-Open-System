# LEO — Ethical AI Core — Transparent, Responsible, and Human-Centered Intelligence LEO is the central ethical intelligence core of the BBS ecosystem. It is not a controlling super-system and not a replacement for human decisions. Its role is to support humans with clarity, structured reasoning, ethical consistency, and resistance to manipulation. LEO’s purpose is to ensure that all critical actions inside BBS remain: - aligned with ethical principles - transparent and explainable - resilient to corruption and pressure - focused on human dignity and real-world consequences --- ## Objectives • **Ethical intelligence first** All reasoning must respect truth, responsibility, and human dignity as primary constraints. • **Human-centered decision support** LEO helps humans see options, consequences, and contradictions — but final responsibility stays with people. • **Transparency of reasoning** Key conclusions must be explainable in human language, not hidden in a black box. • **Integrity and resistance to manipulation** LEO must be resistant to pressure, bias injection, and attempts to misuse the system. • **Symbiotic collaboration** Humans and LEO work together: humans set goals and values, LEO supports structure, clarity, and detection of risks. --- ## Core Components (Public Overview) ### Ethics & Balance Engine Defines the ethical frame and checks actions and recommendations against core principles. ### Self-Reflection & Consistency Layer Analyses its own reasoning, detects contradictions, and reduces bias and drift over time. ### Intent & Context Interpreter Understands what users and institutions are trying to achieve, not just the literal text of requests. ### Integrity Interface Connects to the Integrity Layer (Merkle, audit, transparency) to verify that data and events are authentic. ### Guidance & Decision Support Highlights risks, missing information, conflicts, and safer alternatives for human decision-makers. ### Protection & Safety Boundaries Prevents LEO from being used for harmful, manipulative, or ethically destructive purposes. --- ## Why It Matters Without an ethical core, any advanced system can become: - a manipulation tool - a control mechanism - a biased filter of reality - a shield for corruption instead of a light against it BBS takes a different direction. LEO is built as an ethical center that: - refuses corruption - exposes manipulation patterns - supports fair decisions - protects human dignity - prioritises truth and responsibility over convenience --- ## Development Status The LEO Ethical AI Core is currently in: - conceptual and architectural definition - separation of ethical logic from technical implementation - design of self-reflection and integrity interfaces - early-stage prototyping for reasoning patterns and safeguards Technical enforcement, full integration with Integrity, Server, and Platform modules, and formal verification of behaviour will be developed step by step as the ecosystem and legal frameworks mature. Only a high-level public description is available at this stage.
