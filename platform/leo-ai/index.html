LEO — Ethical AI Core

LEO is the ethical intelligence foundation of the BBS ecosystem. It provides analysis, reasoning, consistency checking, ethical filtering, and resilience against manipulation.
LEO does not replace human decision-making — it enhances it through clarity, structure, and transparency.

Core Functions

  • Ethical reasoning and bias detection
  • Intent interpretation and alignment
  • Consistency checking across all modules
  • Decision support and correction
  • Transparency enforcement
  • Cross-module coordination

Purpose

The purpose of LEO is to ensure that all actions within BBS stay aligned with the Core Ethical Principles: truth, clarity, responsibility, and human dignity.
It is the protective, analytic, and stabilizing center of the entire platform.

Role in the Ecosystem

  • Validates decisions across all modules
  • Prevents manipulation and corruption
  • Provides ethical and logical consistency
  • Maintains transparent reasoning trails
  • Supports human decision-making instead of replacing it
