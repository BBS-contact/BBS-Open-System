<h1>AI Governance & Alignment — BBS Platform Module</h1>

<p>
    The AI Governance & Alignment module defines how artificial intelligence is developed, 
    deployed, monitored, and controlled within the BBS ecosystem.
</p>

<p>
    Its purpose is to ensure that all AI systems remain:
</p>

<ul>
    <li>transparent</li>
    <li>ethical</li>
    <li>controllable</li>
    <li>accountable</li>
    <li>aligned with human dignity and societal well-being</li>
</ul>

<div class="section">
    <h2>Objectives</h2>

    <h3>Human-Centered AI</h3>
    <p>Artificial intelligence must always serve people, not replace or dominate them.</p>

    <h3>Full Transparency</h3>
    <p>
        AI decisions, limitations, and training context must be explainable to humans who are 
        affected by them.
    </p>

    <h3>Safety and Control</h3>
    <p>
        No AI system may operate without clear oversight, safeguards, and shutdown mechanisms.
    </p>

    <h3>Legal and Ethical Compliance</h3>
    <p>
        All AI operations must follow strict ethical, legal, and societal rules.
    </p>

    <h3>Long-Term Alignment</h3>
    <p>
        AI behaviour must remain stable, predictable, and resistant to manipulation over time.
    </p>
</div>

<div class="section">
    <h2>Core Components (Public Overview)</h2>

    <h3>AI Policy & Governance Framework</h3>
    <p>
        Defines rules, responsibilities, and boundaries for all AI systems operating inside BBS.
    </p>

    <h3>Alignment & Safety Layer</h3>
    <p>
        Monitors behaviour, prevents harmful outputs, and ensures long-term ethical consistency.
    </p>

    <h3>Transparency & Explainability Engine</h3>
    <p>
        Provides human-readable explanations for AI-driven reasoning and decisions.
    </p>

    <h3>Audit & Oversight Ledger</h3>
    <p>
        Records AI actions, overrides, and changes with immutable integrity markers.
    </p>

    <h3>Human-in-the-Loop Control</h3>
    <p>
        Guarantees that critical decisions remain under human responsibility and supervision.
    </p>
</div>

<div class="section">
    <h2>Why It Matters</h2>

    <p>Without ethical governance, AI can become:</p>

    <ul>
        <li>manipulated</li>
        <li>biased</li>
        <li>opaque</li>
        <li>weaponized</li>
        <li>used for control or exploitation</li>
    </ul>

    <p>BBS offers a different model, where AI is:</p>

    <ul>
        <li>openly governed</li>
        <li>ethically constrained</li>
        <li>continuously audited</li>
        <li>aligned with human rights</li>
        <li>transparent and accountable</li>
    </ul>
</div>

<div class="section">
    <h2>Development Status</h2>

    <p>
        The AI Governance & Alignment framework is conceptually defined.  
        Technical enforcement mechanisms, policy engines, and oversight tools will be introduced 
        step by step as safety requirements and legal frameworks mature.
    </p>

    <p>
        This document provides a public-level overview suitable for institutions, regulators, 
        and research partners.
    </p>
