Privacy Policy

How BBS collects, processes, and protects data in full compliance with GDPR.

This Privacy Policy applies to the BBS (Big Begins Small) platform as a conceptual and technical initiative. It does not depend on the existence of a registered legal entity and is intended to ensure transparency and compliance with applicable data protection laws.

1. Data We Collect

  • Personal identifiers (e.g. name, email, IP address)
  • Activity logs (interactions with BBS systems)
  • Location and system metadata (only when necessary)
  • Voluntarily submitted content (ideas, forms, projects)

2. Purpose of Data Processing

  • To provide and improve BBS services
  • To ensure system security, transparency, and traceability
  • To allow user interaction and personalization
  • To communicate updates and receive feedback

3. Legal Basis

Data processing is based on user consent, legitimate interest, or contractual necessity, in accordance with Article 6 of the GDPR. Consent may be withdrawn at any time.

4. Data Storage and Protection

Data is stored securely using encryption, access controls, and regular security reviews. No personal data is retained longer than necessary for its intended purpose.

5. Sharing Data

BBS does not sell or trade personal data. Data may be shared only when legally required or with explicit user consent. No tracking, advertising, or profiling is performed.

6. User Rights

  • Right of access
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure (“right to be forgotten”)
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to data portability

To exercise these rights, contact: bbs.contactproton.me@proton.me

7. Cookies

BBS uses only essential cookies required for core functionality. No cookies are used for advertising, tracking, or profiling.

8. Contact

Questions regarding this policy can be directed to: bbs.contactproton.me@proton.me

Updates to this document are published transparently in the Change Log.
