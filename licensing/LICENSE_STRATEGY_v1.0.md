BBS / LEO Licensing Strategy v1.0

Intellectual Property Owner & Author: Pavlo Martseniuk
Authorized Institutional Operator: Fundacja „BBS – Better Balance System”
Year: 2026

1. Overview
LEO is a deterministic institutional system architecture created, designed, and owned exclusively by Pavlo Martseniuk (the Author). 
Fundacja „BBS – Better Balance System” acts as the authorized institutional operator and exclusive licensee under a separate written agreement. 
The system is structured into three distinct licensing layers governed by the Author and managed by the Foundation to ensure architectural integrity, prevent uncontrolled commercial fragmentation, and support sustainable development.

2. Layer I – Public Knowledge Layer
Includes: Conceptual architecture, Governance principles, Ethical framework, High-level module descriptions, Research documentation, Public integrity model explanations.
License: Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 4.0 (CC BY-NC-SA 4.0)
Permitted: Academic use, Educational use, Research publications, Non-commercial adaptation (with appropriate attribution to Pavlo Martseniuk and Fundacja BBS).
Prohibited: Commercial exploitation, Closed commercial redistribution, Fragmentation of system for resale.

3. Layer II – Institutional Integration Layer
Includes: Selected functional modules, API interfaces, Pilot implementations, Research-grade implementations.
Access model: Institutional cooperation agreement required. 
The code in this layer is released under the GNU Affero General Public License v3 (AGPLv3) to ensure total transparency and copyleft protection, preventing closed-source commercial privatization by third parties.
Permitted: Academic institutional research, Pilot testing, Public sector evaluation, Open-source modifications under AGPLv3.
Prohibited without written license: Closed-source commercial deployment, Proprietary SaaS integration, Private monetization.

4. Layer III – Core Runtime & Governance Engine
Includes: Epistemic Core, State Machine, Freeze Pipeline, Cryptographic Signature Layer, Multi-node integrity mechanisms, Identity architecture, Integrity enforcement mechanisms.
Status: Intellectual Property of Pavlo Martseniuk, licensed exclusively to Fundacja BBS for statutory and approved commercial management.
Commercial deployment by third parties requires:
- Explicit written consent from the Author.
- Paid commercial license agreement via Fundacja BBS (with 100% of revenue dedicated to statutory goals and LEO technological development).
- Formal contract and Governance compliance agreement.

5. Non-Fragmentation Principle
LEO may not be split into independent commercial sub-products without written authorization from the Author and the Foundation.

6. Integrity Preservation Clause
Any derivative implementation that materially alters governance integrity principles (the LEO Constitution) may not be represented as LEO.

7. Future Amendments
This licensing structure may evolve under the direct oversight of the Author. The Core Runtime remains protected under this structure unless explicitly modified by a written agreement between the Author and the Foundation.
