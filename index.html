About

Fundacja BBS — Better Balance System is a research-oriented non-profit foundation focused on structured digital governance, transparency-enhancing architectures, and AI risk-aware advisory systems.

The Foundation develops laboratory-tested governance-support models and documentation frameworks aligned with European regulatory standards.

LEO — Logical Epistemic Oversight

LEO is a research initiative operated by the Foundation. It is designed to support:

  • documentation traceability,
  • structured decision-support modeling,
  • AI risk governance frameworks,
  • regulatory alignment within EU law.

LEO functions strictly as an advisory and research system. It does not replace institutional authority and does not issue binding decisions.

Regulatory Context

Development activities consider alignment with:

  • GDPR (Regulation (EU) 2016/679),
  • EU Artificial Intelligence Act,
  • EU data governance frameworks,
  • Polish foundation and accounting law.
