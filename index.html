Overview

LEO System Map

The LEO architecture follows a structured analytical pipeline designed to detect institutional anomalies and support investigative workflows.

Truth Ingestion
Structured acquisition of verified information.
Anomaly Analysis
Graph analysis and pattern detection engines.
Investigation Pipeline
Automated investigation reports and case building.
Institutional Alerts
Risk escalation and institutional notification.

LEO (Logical Epistemic Oversight) is a research-oriented governance support architecture designed to explore structured anomaly detection, institutional transparency and compliance-aware AI advisory systems.

The system operates as a research and advisory infrastructure and does not replace institutional authority or issue legally binding decisions.
