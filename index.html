LEO – Logical Epistemic Oversight

Developed by Fundacja BBS – Better Balance System
Jurisdiction: Poland (European Union)

Overview

LEO (Logical Epistemic Oversight) is a research-oriented governance support architecture developed by Fundacja BBS. The project explores structured anomaly detection, institutional transparency and compliance-aware AI advisory systems.

The system operates as a research and advisory infrastructure and does not replace institutional authority or issue legally binding decisions.
