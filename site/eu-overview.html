BBS — European Commission Overview

This page provides a high-level institutional overview of the BBS ecosystem for EU-level evaluation, including the European Commission, JRC, STOA, and related digital governance bodies.

What is BBS?

BBS (Big Begins Small) is an ethical, transparent, modular digital ecosystem designed to strengthen public trust, eliminate corruption, and provide a verifiable integrity backbone for modern societies.

It is not a commercial platform and not a governmental IT system. It is a public infrastructure model integrating:

  • ethical AI (LEO)
  • tamper-proof audit mechanisms
  • transparent digital governance tools
  • open societal modules covering essential public services

Purpose of EU Cooperation

BBS is presented to the EU as a next-generation integrity and transparency framework, suitable for:

  • pilot testing in public institutions
  • cross-border digital cooperation
  • ethics-first AI deployment
  • early-stage infrastructure research
