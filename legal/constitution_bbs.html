Statute (Public Extract)

Institutional framework and governing principles

1. Legal Status

Fundacja BBS — Better Balance System ("Foundation") is a Polish foundation established and operating under the Act on Foundations and other applicable laws of the Republic of Poland.

The Foundation is entered into the National Court Register (KRS) under number 0001220296 and operates as a non-profit legal entity.

2. Mission

The mission of the Foundation is to conduct and support academic, research, analytical, and technological activities in the field of governance systems, institutional integrity, public-sector innovation, and digital accountability frameworks.

The Foundation operates in a research and laboratory capacity, including simulation-based governance modeling and analytical system development.

3. Scope of Activities

  • Research and development of governance-support systems
  • Academic cooperation and analytical publications
  • Educational initiatives and knowledge dissemination
  • Development of institutional transparency tools

The Foundation does not exercise public authority and does not replace governmental institutions. All system outputs are advisory and analytical in nature.

4. Governance Structure

The Foundation is governed by its Management Board (Zarząd) in accordance with the Statute and Polish law.

The detailed internal structure, appointment rules, and competences are defined in the official Statute filed with the National Court Register.

5. Financial Principles

The Foundation operates on a non-profit basis. All revenues are allocated exclusively to statutory objectives.

Financial records are maintained in accordance with Polish accounting standards and internal financial governance policies.

6. Official Version

This page constitutes a public informational extract. The legally binding version of the Statute is the signed and registered document filed with the National Court Register.

In case of discrepancy, the official Polish-language Statute prevails.
