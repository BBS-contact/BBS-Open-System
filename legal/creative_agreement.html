Creative Freedom Agreement

This Agreement defines the terms under which authors publish their works on the BBS Creative Module.

1. Purpose

The purpose of this Agreement is to ensure that creative works published through the BBS Creative Module are protected, transparent, and accessible while respecting both freedom of expression and ethical standards.

2. Rights of Authors

  • Authors retain full copyright and ownership of their works.
  • Authors may remove or update their works at any time.
  • BBS provides free access to publish without censorship, except in cases defined under responsibilities below.

3. Responsibilities of Authors

Authors agree not to submit works that:

  • Promote hate, discrimination, or violence.
  • Violate laws or intellectual property rights of others.
  • Contain harmful, exploitative, or abusive content.

4. BBS Platform’s Role

BBS acts as a neutral host and transparency layer. It does not edit, alter, or censor works, except where strictly required by law or this Agreement. BBS provides verification tools and ensures that every submission is included in the public transparency log.

5. Transparency & Public Record

Each creative submission is registered in the BBS Transparency Portal through an immutable audit log (Merkle root). This guarantees authenticity, prevents manipulation, and provides a verifiable public record of publication.

6. Liability Disclaimer

BBS is not liable for the misuse of published works by third parties, to the extent permitted by applicable law. Responsibility lies solely with the author for the content they provide.

BBS ensures transparency but does not act as publisher or editor.

7. Acceptance

By checking the consent box in the Creative Module submission form, the author confirms that they have read, understood, and accepted this Agreement.

This Agreement becomes binding upon acceptance, to the extent permitted by applicable law.
