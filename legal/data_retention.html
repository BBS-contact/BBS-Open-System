Data Retention Policy

Retention periods, deletion standards, and archival governance

1. Purpose

This Policy defines the principles governing retention, archiving, and deletion of data processed by Fundacja BBS — Better Balance System ("Foundation").

The Policy is implemented in alignment with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR), applicable Polish legislation, and the Foundation’s internal governance framework.

2. General Principles

  • Data is retained only for clearly defined and lawful purposes;
  • Retention periods are proportionate to statutory, contractual, or regulatory obligations;
  • Data minimization and storage limitation principles apply at all times;
  • Secure deletion or anonymization is performed once retention is no longer justified.

3. Categories and Retention Periods

Retention periods vary depending on data category and legal basis:

  • Contractual and cooperation data: retained for the duration of cooperation and subsequent limitation periods under civil law;
  • Accounting and financial documentation: retained in accordance with Polish accounting and tax regulations;
  • Grant and project documentation: retained for periods required by funding agreements or EU regulations;
  • Technical logs and security records: retained proportionately for system integrity, security monitoring, and legal compliance;
  • Email correspondence: retained only as necessary for operational or legal purposes.

4. Archiving and Anonymization

Where historical documentation is preserved for research, audit, or transparency purposes, personal identifiers are removed or anonymized unless continued retention is required by law.

Archival records are stored with appropriate technical and organizational safeguards.

5. Right to Erasure

Data subjects have the right to request erasure of personal data in accordance with Article 17 GDPR, subject to statutory retention obligations or overriding legal grounds.

Deletion requests are assessed and processed without undue delay.

6. Secure Deletion

The Foundation applies appropriate technical measures to ensure secure deletion or irreversible anonymization of data once retention periods expire.

7. Review and Updates

This Policy is reviewed periodically and may be updated to reflect regulatory or operational changes.

The binding framework remains applicable EU and Polish law.
