Data Retention & Deletion Policy

How BBS stores, archives, and deletes data in compliance with GDPR and ethical standards.

1. General Principles

BBS respects the fundamental right to privacy and data protection. Data is retained only as long as necessary for transparency, accountability, or user services.

2. Retention Periods

  • User Data: Retained only for the duration of participation or consent.
  • Audit Logs: Retained in anonymized form where necessary to ensure system integrity, security, and legal accountability, in accordance with applicable law.
  • Temporary Data: Deleted automatically after processing.

3. Right to Deletion

Every user has the right to request erasure of their personal data (“right to be forgotten”). Requests will be processed without undue delay, except where retention is legally required.

4. Data Archiving

Historical records may be anonymized and archived for research, transparency, or statistical purposes. No personal identifiers are preserved in archives.

5. Updates

This policy may evolve. All updates are recorded transparently in the Change Log.
