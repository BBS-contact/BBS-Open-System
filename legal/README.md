# Legal Documentation — Fundacja BBS — Better Balance System

This directory contains the public legal documentation of
Fundacja BBS — Better Balance System,
a Polish foundation registered in the National Court Register (KRS 0001220296).

These documents provide transparency regarding the Foundation’s:

- legal status,
- governance framework,
- data protection practices,
- AI governance standards,
- intellectual property rules.

The legally binding document is the official Polish-language Statute
filed with the National Court Register.

This repository publishes public-facing versions of legal and governance
documents for transparency and institutional review purposes.

## Structure

- legal.html — Legal overview and identity
- statute.html — Public extract aligned with the registered Statute
- terms-of-use.html — Website terms of use
- privacy-policy.html — GDPR-aligned privacy policy
- ai_policy.html — AI governance policy (EU AI Act aligned)
- data_retention.html — Data retention and deletion framework
- copyright.html — Intellectual property notice

## Scope

These documents are informational and transparency-oriented.
They do not replace statutory law or official regulatory acts.

Applicable law: Republic of Poland and European Union legislation.
