# BBS — Legal Framework

This directory contains the public legal and ethical framework of the
**BBS (Big Begins Small)** initiative.

These documents define the principles, boundaries, and governance logic
under which the BBS system is designed and developed.

They are published to ensure:
- transparency,
- accountability,
- ethical clarity,
- alignment with European fundamental values.

---

## Scope

The documents in this directory represent:
- a **conceptual legal framework**,
- an **ethical governance model**,
- a **compliance-oriented system design**.

They do **not** constitute binding law, legal advice, or an official
jurisdiction.

---

## Structure

- `legal.html` — Entry point and overview of the legal status
- `constitution_bbs.html` — Foundational constitutional principles
- `privacy_policy.html` — Data protection and privacy principles (GDPR-aligned)
- `terms_of_use.html` — Rules for platform usage
- `ai_policy.html` — AI ethics, transparency, and human oversight
- `security_principles.html` — Security and integrity foundations
- `change_log.html` — Transparent record of document changes
- `status_eu.md` — European Union alignment statement

---

## Core Principles

- Human dignity over automation
- Transparency over opacity
- Accountability over authority
- Human oversight over algorithmic autonomy
- Public interest over private manipulation

---

## Notice

Internal compliance engines, decision logic, enforcement rules, and
anti-manipulation mechanisms are part of the protected BBS core and
are **not published** in this repository.

This separation is intentional and fundamental to the system’s integrity.
